# Guangzhou health check for foreigners: what to confirm before booking

Guangzhou arrivals may need to separate official visa or residence requirements from private health screening options. Planning before arrival helps prevent wrong bookings and missed documents.

## Plan before arrival if possible

If your health check is connected to a work visa, residence permit, school registration, or employer onboarding, do not assume any hospital package will work. Start by confirming the exact purpose and whether a designated center, certificate format, or local verification process is required.

If your goal is a private full body checkup, your planning questions are different. You should compare package scope, price, English support, report language, and follow-up options before paying.

## Documents to confirm

Confirm passport details, photos, school or employer notices, existing overseas medical forms, and whether report pickup or verification is required. Requirements can differ by purpose and local execution.

Before booking in Guangzhou, prepare a simple checklist:

- Passport and current visa or entry details
- Employer, school, or residence permit notice if applicable
- Any overseas health report or vaccination document you want reviewed
- Target date and deadline
- Preferred language for registration, report explanation, and follow-up

## When English support matters

Even when a process is straightforward for locals, foreigners may struggle with registration, payment, report pickup, and follow-up questions. English planning support can reduce wrong bookings and missed documents.

English support is especially useful when you need to ask whether an overseas report can be accepted, whether a private package includes an English report, or what to do if the report has abnormal flags.
